Ongoing Campaigns

Blood Donation

Anyone who is eligible for blood donation is heartly requested to come forward and donate to save lives. It is necessary to be in good health to donate blood.

Free Health Checkup

It can be availed by anyone attending the camp. You are required to bring you Aadhar Card for id verification.

First Aid workshop

In this workshop, our experts will guide on the do's and don'ts during First Aid. It will allow everyone to learn basic first aid tricks and apply them whenever needed.

Our Vision

We aim to create awareness and ensure that even all the underpriviledged are called 'Healthy citizens of India'. We try to provide free medicines to the needy, give appropriate medical advices, provide support for surgeries and control the health issue before it turns severe.


Location

Benefits

Certificate to all

Every attedee will be provided with a Certificate by ACM. Blood donation, medical checkup or first aid workshop, wherever you took part will be mentioned along.

Free Expert Advice

Our Experts are always ready to offer clear guidance on any doubt you might be facing related to your health. You can ask for referals if required.

Enhanced Knowledge

Attending such camps, will expose you to basic Knowledge about your body and health. Also, the first aid workshop is a great learning experience.

Our Journey

We started off 4-years back in June 2018, and it has been a full-flegded meeting with new people routine for our volunteers. We have been researching along currently.

We started off small, fought and help extensively during covid, left off with so many happy faces, got recognised and were even awarded with 'Best Heath Help during Covid' award by The Government of India. Now, we tend to keep working and help as much as we can.
